WELCOME To Bella's Pizza

Bella Pizzeria offers delicious pizza and more! Whether you want pizza, subs, calzones, appetizers, or drinks - Bella Pizzeria is the place for you! We are a family business and have been serving North Billerica since 1991! Our dining room seats 50, so come with a crowd! You'll be sure to have a great time! Some of our favorite dishes are: meat lovers pizza, calzones, mozzarella sticks, and the french fries! We begin every day making fresh dough and custom sauces. This ensures the freshest quality product. You can order our pizza online and take out as well!
